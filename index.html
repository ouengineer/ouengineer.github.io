Hi, I'm Matthew Thomas...

I'm an Austin-based entrepreneur. I've spent most of my career working in start-ups with a particular focus on #EdTech SaaS companies. Most recently I co-founded AcademicWorks, which provides cloud-based software to help colleges, universities, and community foundations manage their scholarship processes while increasing donor engagement. We help ensure that these institutions use all their scholarship funds while honoring their donors' wishes. That's a fancy way of saying that we help encourage the virtuous cycle ensuring that scholarship dollars are used to help kids pay for college while recognizing the donors for their impact and contribution so that they and their friends will be inclined to help more students.

In April 2017, AcademicWorks was acquired by Blackbaud (NASDAQ: BLKB).

Outside of entrepreneurial endeavors, you'll usually find me running around Lady Bird Lake or fighting my way through a marathon with Nutella to make up for the homebrew I just finished. You're also likely see me at an Entrepreneurs' Organization event, which always seems to be filled with really fantastic and inspiring people.
